🧾 PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Product Name: LedgerLite (working name)
1. 🧠 Product Overview
Problem

Small businesses in Nigeria/Africa:

Still use notebooks or Excel
Don’t track profit properly
Lose money through poor debt tracking
Can’t generate financial reports

These are explicit pain points Luca solves (manual tracking, lack of insights)

Solution

A simple, mobile-first web app that helps SMEs:

Track money (income & expenses)
Manage customers & debts
Generate invoices/receipts
See business performance in real-time
2. 🎯 Target Users
Primary Users
Small business owners (retail, POS shops, fashion, food vendors)
Freelancers (designers, developers)
Side hustlers
Secondary Users
Accountants managing multiple SMEs
Sales staff (multi-user access)
